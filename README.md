# Whetstone Entertainment

Atlanta's premier music management company representing visionary artists, songwriters and producers shaping the sound of tomorrow.

## About

Whetstone Entertainment provides hands-on management, A&R direction, project execution, and deal strategy for Grammy-winning producers and rising innovators. Built on 15+ years of industry experience.
