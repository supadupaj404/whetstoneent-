Who We Are

Elite Music Management

Whetstone Entertainment represents and develops the creators shaping tomorrow's sound. Rooted in Atlanta and built on 15+ years of experience, we provide hands on management, A&R direction, project execution, and deal strategy for Grammy winning producers and rising innovators.

We operate at the intersection of culture, business, and technology, helping our clients navigate the shifting landscape of rights, distribution, and emerging AI tools. Our mission is to turn world class creative output into long term, defensible careers.
